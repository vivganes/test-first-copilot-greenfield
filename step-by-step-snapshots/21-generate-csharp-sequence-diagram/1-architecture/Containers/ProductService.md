# Product Service

## Description
The Product Service manages the product catalog, including product creation, updates, categorization, variants, images, and brand associations. It supports product search, filtering, and low-stock alerts. Sellers use this service to manage their listings, while customers and admins access product data for browsing and management.

## Communication
- Communicates with: Inventory Service, Promotion Service, Review & Rating Service, API Gateway, Storefront UI, Seller Dashboard UI, Admin UI.
